Routines

Morning: Gentle cleanser → Vitamin C serum (10–15%) → Lightweight moisturizer → SPF 30–50.
Evening: Cleanser → Niacinamide (4–5%) or Hyaluronic acid serum → Moisturizer.
Weekly: 1× enzyme peel + 1× hydrating mask.

Morning: Creamy cleanser → Hyaluronic acid + Panthenol serum → Ceramide + Squalane moisturizer → SPF 30–50.
Evening: Gentle cleanser → Ceramide/Niacinamide serum → Rich moisturizer + optional occlusive.
Weekly: 1–2× hydrating mask. Avoid alcohol & harsh surfactants.

Morning: Foaming gel cleanser → Niacinamide (4–10%) or Zinc serum → Gel moisturizer → Matte SPF 30–50.
Evening: Cleanser → BHA (Salicylic acid 0.5–2%) every other night → Lightweight moisturizer.
Weekly: Clay mask on T-zone once. Don’t skip moisturizer!

Morning: Gel cleanser → Niacinamide serum → Moisturizer (light on T-zone, richer on cheeks) → SPF 30–50.
Evening: Cleanser → BHA on T-zone, HA/Ceramide serum on cheeks → Moisturizer.
Weekly: Multi-masking (clay on T-zone, hydration mask on cheeks).

Morning: Mild fragrance-free cleanser → Panthenol + Centella serum → Ceramide moisturizer → Mineral SPF 30–50.
Evening: Gentle cleanser → Azulene/Allantoin/β-glucan serum → Ceramide cream.
Avoid: Fragrance, alcohol, menthol, strong acids, hot water.

Morning: Gel cleanser → Niacinamide or Azelaic acid (10%) serum → Lightweight moisturizer → SPF 30–50.
Evening: Alternate nights → (A) BHA 1–2% or (B) Retinoid (Adapalene/Retinol) → Moisturizer.
Spot treatment: Benzoyl Peroxide 2.5% on blemishes only.

Morning: Gentle cleanser → Vitamin C (10–15%) serum → Ceramide + Peptide moisturizer → SPF 50.
Evening: Alternate nights → (A) Retinoid (Retinal/Retinol) + Barrier Cream / (B) AHA 5–8% or PHA → Moisturizer.
Weekly: Hydrating or peptide mask; gentle exfoliation.
